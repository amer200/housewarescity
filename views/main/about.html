Welcome to Al Awani Houseware City Industry

Al Awani Houseware City Industry , was established in 2020 in Jeddah, affiliated with the Al-Jahoury Group for Industry and Trade. We specialize in the manufacture and production of utensils and household appliances. We work on using the latest production technology and the best raw materials. Al-Awan City Co. proves itself well as one of the best Saudi factories specialized in this field. Through our experience, we have a distinguished position in the Saudi market.

Mission

Improve household utensils products by examining raw materials and meticulous follow-up of production stages to provide high quality products. We are proud to present these products to the Saudi community to be part of its economy.

Vision

Maintaining the company's distinguished level in the local market, we believe development and modern technology are significant to success and distinction. We invest in this field to achieve leadership in manufacturing household utensils.

Our message

To provide our customers with high-quality products that meet their needs at competitive prices and manage our brand by investing in our human resources and using the best technology in the industry to maintain our image as a reliable factory.
